\begin{frame}{Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU)}

The IMU consists of two sensors providing \textbf{physical quantities}:
\begin{itemize}
\item Accelerometer: linear acceleration ($m/s^2$);
\item Gyroscope: angular velocity ($rad/s$).
\end{itemize}

\end{frame}

\begin{itemize}

\item Attitude.
\end{itemize}
